Welcome to Pixel Poetry, my personal sanctuary where I delve into the profound world's of pieces of digital art , seeking the raw emotions and intellectual appreciation they offer while also covering some general informations about them.

What you'll mainly find here are my musings, insights, hot takes and heartfelt reflections on video games, anime, movies and anything that have left a profound impact on me.

Feel free to explore the pages, share your own stories, and embark on this journey with me. You can contact me here at @fantomepoetry@gmail.com feel free to make any suggestions or share your thoughts.
